About Kyle

Kyle Lambert is an award-winning illustrator specializing in movie posters and entertainment artwork. His iconic posters for Stranger Things have become cultural phenomena, blending traditional painting techniques with digital tools.

With over 15 years of experience, Kyle has created artwork for major studios including Netflix, Disney, Universal, and Warner Bros.
